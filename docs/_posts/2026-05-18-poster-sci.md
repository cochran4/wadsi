Another research poster was presented at the 2026 Society for Causal Inference Conference (Salt Lake City, Utah). This one focuses on the methods used to study early-life factors and autism. Because researchers cannot conduct experiments during pregnancy or early childhood, understanding these relationships requires careful use of observational data and explicit assumptions. The protocol introduces a transparent, community-informed framework for comparing multiple factors within a single analysis and for interpreting what conclusions can—and cannot—be drawn from the results.
